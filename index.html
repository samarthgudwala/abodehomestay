Frequently Asked Questions

01

Do you provide breakfast?

No, breakfast facilities are not available on the premises. However tea and coffee machine is available on charged basis for self-service. Ample of cafes and restaurants in the vicinity may be used.

02

Is parking available?

Parking space on a common street is available. No provision of parking within the premises.

03

Are there any time restrictions?

No, there are no time constraints, premises remain open 24/7.
Check-out timings are fixed (11am).

04

Are housekeeping services present?

Housekeeping services can be availed once a day at 11am daily for cleaning, except that there are no other services provided.
Kitchenette is accessible on self-service basis.
